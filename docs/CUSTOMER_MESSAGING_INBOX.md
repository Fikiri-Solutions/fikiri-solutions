# Customer messaging — Inbox & Command Center

Use this language in sales, onboarding, and in-product copy.

## Say

- **AI-assisted inbox organization and lead capture**
- Synced mail is **classified into tabs** (Leads, Action Needed, Review Queue, etc.)
- **You approve** archive, dismiss, labels, and destructive actions
- **Chatbot** answers from your knowledge base and can capture leads when configured
- **Review Queue** for uncertain classifications

## Do not say

- “Zero hallucinations” / “100% accurate”
- “Fully autonomous inbox” / “AI runs your inbox for you”
- “Auto-delete spam” (delete/spam require explicit confirmation)
- “Replaces Gmail” (Live mail tab is still Gmail; Command Center uses synced copies)

## Short elevator (one sentence)

Fikiri helps you **organize synced email with AI triage**, **capture leads**, and **run an AI chatbot** on your content—with you in control of what gets archived, dismissed, or removed.
