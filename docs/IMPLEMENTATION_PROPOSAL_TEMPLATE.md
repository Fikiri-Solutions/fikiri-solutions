## 4. Package 2 — Foundations Sprint

**Purpose:** Make **CRM and email** trustworthy for automation: one source of truth, clear ownership, and a baseline so we do not automate broken data or ambiguous rules.

**Prerequisite:** Package 1 complete (or equivalent diagnostic approved in writing).  
**Calendar:** Typically `2–3 weeks`.  
**Internal effort (reference only):** Often `15–35` hours depending on current state; your price is fixed below.

### Tangible deliverables (you receive)

1. **CRM truth rules** — Documented: stages, ownership, deduplication approach (what counts as the same lead/contact).  
2. **Email operational baseline** — Where inbound mail is handled, routing principles, template locations, and “where work lives” so nothing critical stays only in personal inboxes.  
3. **Workflow document** — Single written workflow for the chosen process (roles, triggers, exceptions at high level).  
4. **Minimal reporting** — `3–5` KPIs tied to this workflow (definitions + where they are viewed), so you can see if the process is healthy.

## 5. Package 3 — Automation Build Sprint

**Purpose:** Ship **one** workflow end-to-end on Fikiri: build, test on real examples, train your team, handoff checklist, rollback plan, and **30-day stabilization** for issues tied to this scope.

**Prerequisite:** Package 2 complete for this workflow (unless you have a signed waiver accepting automation risk on unclean data—we do not recommend this).

**Calendar:** Typically `2–4 weeks` build + `30-day` stabilization window.  
**Internal effort (reference only):** Often `20–60` hours depending on integrations and exceptions; your price is fixed below.

### Tangible deliverables (you receive)

1. **Production automation** — The scoped workflow live in your environment, with configuration documented at a practical level.  
2. **Test evidence** — Walkthrough on `[N]` real or realistic examples showing expected behavior.  
3. **Training** — Session(s) for named roles: `[roles]`; short reference notes or checklist agreed in advance.  
4. **Handoff checklist** — What you own ongoing (e.g., who adjusts rules, who approves CRM changes).  
5. **Rollback / pause plan** — How to safely pause or revert the automation if needed.  
6. **30-day stabilization** — Fixes and tuning **within the agreed scope** for issues discovered in production during that window.
