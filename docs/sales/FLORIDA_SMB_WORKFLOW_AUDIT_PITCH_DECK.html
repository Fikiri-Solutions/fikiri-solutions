The real cost of manual workflows

  • Slow first response — prospect calls the next company on Google
  • No follow-up cadence — quotes sit until they go cold
  • Duplicate records — team contacts the same person twice (or not at all)
  • Owner as router — growth hits a ceiling around 20–40 employees
  • Tool sprawl — inbox, sheets, CRM, group chat — nothing agrees
