# Follow-Up & Proposal Messaging — Fikiri Solutions

Copy, personalize placeholders `[like this]`, send. Keep subject lines under ~60 characters where possible.

## 1. Proposal summary template *(internal or client-facing email)*

**Use after:** Consultation completed; you’re sending or attaching the formal proposal.

---

**Subject:** Summary — Fikiri recommendation for [Business Name]

---

Hi [Contact Name],

Thanks again for walking us through how work moves from **[first touch]** to **[getting paid]**.

**What we heard**  
[One short paragraph in plain English—the operational pain, no jargon.]

**Where the bottleneck is**  
[Bottleneck in terms of delay, leakage, manual work, or risk—pick primary.]

**What we recommend**  
- **Phase 1:** [Concrete outcome—e.g., auto-route inquiries, SLA visibility, single record of truth for X]  
- **Phase 2 (after Phase 1 is stable):** [Optional]  

**Investment (per proposal)**  
| | |
|--|--|
| One-time setup | [range or fixed] |
| Monthly | [range or fixed] / month |

**Why this tier**  
We’re sizing this as **[Entry / Standard / Complex]** because [2 short bullets—volume, systems, complexity, urgency].

**Next step**  
[Pick one: reply with questions · book 20 min to lock scope · sign by DATE · send sample export]

Best,  
[Your name]  
Fikiri Solutions  
[Phone] · [Email]

## 2. Follow-up message — after consultation, no proposal yet

**Subject:** Next steps — Fikiri × [Business Name]

---

Hi [Contact Name],

Good speaking with you today. Quick recap:

- **Workflow we mapped:** [one line]  
- **Themes:** [delay / manual steps / leakage / risk — keep short]  

I’m putting together **[a short written recommendation / numbers]** and will send it by **[date]**.

If anything shifts on your side (timeline, tools, volume), reply to this email and I’ll factor it in.

Best,  
[Your name]

---

## 3. Follow-up — proposal sent, awaiting reply

**Subject:** Did you get the proposal? — Fikiri

---

Hi [Contact Name],

I sent the proposal on **[date]** — wanted to make sure it landed and see if you had questions.

**Three ways we can move:**  
1. **15-minute call** to walk through scope — [calendar link]  
2. **Reply with edits** if something’s off on timeline or stack  
3. **Hold** — if now isn’t the time, tell me and I’ll check back **[month/quarter]**

No pressure either way; we only want this if it’s clearly worth it for you.

Best,  
[Your name]

---

## 4. Follow-up — nurture (long cycle)

**Subject:** Still automating [workflow piece]? — Fikiri

---

Hi [Contact Name],

We talked **[timeframe]** about **[pain in their words]**. No rush—if this is still on your radar this quarter, I’m happy to **[15-min catch-up / refreshed one-pager / revised estimate]**.

If priorities moved, a one-line “not now” saves us both follow-ups.

Best,  
[Your name]

---

## 5. Won — kickoff confirmation

**Subject:** Next steps — welcome aboard, [Business Name]

---

Hi [Contact Name],

Great — we’re aligned to start **[Phase 1 scope in one sentence]**.

**What happens next**  
1. **[Deposit / contract / access]** — **[deadline or link]**  
2. **Kickoff call** — **[date/time]** — attendees: **[roles]**  
3. **You’ll receive** — **[what they need to prepare: exports, logins via secure method, etc.]**

Reach out anytime before kickoff if something changes.

Best,  
[Your name]

---

## 6. Lost / not qualified — graceful close

**Subject:** Thanks — Fikiri & [Business Name]

---

Hi [Contact Name],

Thanks for the transparency on **[budget / timing / fit]**. Based on that, we’re **[pausing / not moving forward]** for now.

If **[volume picks up / stack stabilizes / season changes]**, you’re welcome to ping me—I’m glad to revisit.

Wishing you a strong **[season / quarter].**

Best,  
[Your name]

## 7. SMS — short follow-up *(use only with consent / appropriate relationship)*

**Examples (≤300 chars target):**

- Hi [Name] — [Your name] from Fikiri. Sent a short recap to [email]. Any questions on timing? [First name only opt-out if required]
- [Name], following up on our chat about [pain]. Open to a 15-min call this week? [link]
