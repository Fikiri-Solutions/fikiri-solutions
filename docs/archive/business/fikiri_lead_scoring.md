# Fikiri Solutions — Lead Scoring (0–100)

**Purpose:** Prioritize who to call back first and how hard to push—without pretending it’s science. Calibrate quarterly against **win rate** and **deal quality**.

**Rule:** Score **after** intake or first call when you have signal. Re-score when budget, urgency, or workflow clarity changes.

---

## How the 100 points split

| Category | Max points | What it measures |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| **A. Fit & workflow clarity** | 25 | Can we map Input → Decision → Execution → Follow-Up → Money? |
| **B. Volume & impact** | 25 | Enough throughput for ROI on automation. |
| **C. Budget & readiness** | 20 | Can they pay and implement? |
| **D. Digital baseline** | 15 | Data and tools won’t fight us every day. |
| **E. Urgency & access** | 15 | Timeline + decision-maker access. |

**Total:** 100

---

## A. Fit & workflow clarity (0–25)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 20–25 | One primary workflow named; owners know steps; clear pain; realistic automation boundary. |
| 12–19 | Workflow understandable but messy or multi-threaded; needs diagnostic to narrow scope. |
| 5–11 | Vague (“everything’s broken”) or scope explosion; high discovery tax. |
| 0–4 | No owner for process, or expectation is “AI replaces staff” with no process. |

---

## B. Volume & impact (0–25)

Use **weekly items through the workflow** (leads, jobs, loads, orders—not vanity traffic).

| Score | Weekly volume (typical) |
|-------|-------------------------|
| 22–25 | 51+ / week **or** huge margin per unit (explain in notes). |
| 16–21 | 16–50 / week. |
| 10–15 | 5–15 / week. |
| 4–9 | Under 5 / week **but** high ticket or regulated (still justify in notes). |
| 0–3 | Too sparse to justify build unless pilot scope is tiny **and** they accept it. |

---

## C. Budget & readiness (0–20)

| Score | Signals |
|-------|---------|
| 17–20 | Budget discussed without flinch; prefers phased OK; paying for software today. |
| 11–16 | Range fits your Standard tier; needs ROI framing but willing. |
| 5–10 | “Find savings first”; heavy price sensitivity; many free tools only. |
| 0–4 | No budget path; ghost on commercial questions; wants free automation only. |

---

## D. Digital baseline (0–15)

Map roughly to **Digital Infrastructure Score** (1–5) on the CRM record:

| Infra score (1–5) | Points |
|-------------------|--------|
| 5 — Single system of record, exports OK, light integrations | 13–15 |
| 4 — Messy but one dominant tool + sheets | 10–12 |
| 3 — Spreadsheet truth + email chaos | 6–9 |
| 2 — WhatsApp / paper / tribal only | 3–5 |
| 1 — Hostile to change; no admin | 0–2 |

---

## E. Urgency & access (0–15)

| Score | Signals |
|-------|---------|
| 13–15 | Start within 30 days; decision-maker on calls; clear deadline (season, contract). |
| 8–12 | 1–3 months; DM loops through office manager (slow but workable). |
| 4–7 | Exploring; multi-owner veto risk. |
| 0–3 | No timeline; “just researching”; cannot reach decision maker. |

---

## Weighted shortcut (5-minute version)

If you don’t want to itemize, use gut buckets:

1. Rate each A–E **1–5** (five stars conceptually).  
2. **Weighted sum:**  
   `Score = A×5 + B×5 + C×4 + D×3 + E×3`  
   - A max 25, B max 25, C max 20, D max 15, E max 15 → **normalize to 100** if needed, or cap raw sum at 100.

**Simpler:** Multiply average star rating × 20 → **0–100** if you rate overall fit 1–5 stars (quick triage only).

---

## How to use the number

| Range | Typical action |
|-------|----------------|
| **75–100** | Fast follow-up; sharp proposal; protect calendar for build. |
| **55–74** | Standard nurture; clear next step and date. |
| **35–54** | Long-cycle; education content; revisit quarterly. |
| **Under 35** | Nurture or **Not Qualified** unless strategic (portfolio, referral source). |

**Never:** Treat score as moral judgment—use it for **ordering your week**.
